Markdown vs. Plain Text for AI

Which format should you choose for your prompts? It depends on your goal:

  • Markdown: Best for complex instructions. Use this to maintain your hierarchy of bullet points and headers, helping the AI understand relationships between ideas.
  • Plain Text: Best for simple data ingestion. Use our plain text converter when you need to strip all styling and reduce your token count to the absolute minimum.
