The Fastest Way to Convert Word to Markdown

When you copy-paste from Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or OneNote directly into AI chat boxes like ChatGPT or Claude, you often lose your list hierarchy. NoteToPrompt solves this by acting as a high-fidelity rich text to markdown converter. We strip the hidden HTML styling and replace it with clean, AI-native Markdown syntax.

Why Use Markdown Instead of Plain Text?

While many users look for a plain text converter to remove formatting, Markdown is superior for Prompt Engineering. AI models like GPT-4 and Claude 3.5 are trained on vast amounts of web data where Markdown is the standard. By using dashes (-) for bullets, you help the AI understand your instruction hierarchy.
