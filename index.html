Why NoteToPrompt?

Directly pasting from Google Docs or Microsoft Word often loses nested indentation and bullet hierarchy. NoteToPrompt converts your rich text into clean Markdown format—the native language of AI models—ensuring ChatGPT or Claude understands exactly how your ideas are grouped.

Pro Tip: Use indents to show "Child" ideas. NoteToPrompt will turn them into nested dashes for the AI.
