Why convert Word to Markdown for AI?

When you copy from Word or Google Docs, you copy hidden HTML. AI models like ChatGPT and Claude 3.5 struggle with this, often ignoring indents. Markdown uses clean symbols (- and #) that AI understands perfectly.

How do I clean text for ChatGPT?

Simply paste your formatted notes into the left box. NoteToPrompt strips away the rich text formatting and generates clean Markdown text that preserves your bullet points and indentation.

Why use Markdown for Prompt Engineering?

Markdown provides a clear hierarchy. Using Markdown for context allows AI models to follow complex instructions 30% more accurately by defining clear relationships between data points.
